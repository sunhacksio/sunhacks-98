sunhacks 2021 FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • what is a hackathon?
    • 24-36 hour prototyping marathon (we are 36 hours). Work on softwareor hardware solutions to real world problems, innovative projects, or anything you can think of!
  • who can hack?
    • For the online portions of the hackathon, anybody and everybody over the age of 18 can participate! However, only Arizona State University students can attend the in-person portion of sunhacks.
  • i've never programmed before, is it okay?
    • Yes! Programmers are not the only people who can participate at a hackathon. We've had designers, business people, and all sorts of people attend sunhacks. If you are new to programming, we also have mentors and workshops that can teach you how to code!
  • how much does it cost to participate?
    • sunhacks costs zip, zilch and nada to participate! We will never charge you any sort of participation fee.
  • what are the rules?
    • Follow the MLH code of conduct. 1-4 members to a team
  • can I be a mentor/judge/speaker?
    • All are eligible to be a mentor or speaker regardless of prior experience (though you should be 18 or older). If you are a hacker you cannot be a judge though. As we have limited space for speakers and judges, those will be selected based on talk or experience respectively and you will receive confirmation from us when it comes time.
